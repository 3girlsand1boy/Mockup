Currently, WOL has a steady stream of volunteers with a blend of multi-ethnic and committed workers for its daily program operation. The volunteers assist in receives deliveries, taking inventory, stocking food, and handling food distribution. Schools, corporations, churches, local businesses, and community residents provide this extra help 7 days a week. Though our volunteers come from different walks of life, they all share our passion to feed the poor and eliminate hunger in our community. New York Coalition Against Hunger (NYCCAH) has been very helpful in finding volunteers for our program.
